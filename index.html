About Us

ApeKade is an online shopping store created to bring useful and trending products to customers in Sri Lanka. We offer LED lights, drinkware, home essentials, kitchen tools, toys, beauty care items, smart gadgets and special offers.

Our goal is to make shopping simple, safe and convenient. Customers can browse products, add items to cart and place orders easily through WhatsApp.

Return Policy

We accept return requests only for damaged, wrong or defective products. Customers must inform us within 24 hours after receiving the product.

To request a return, customers should send clear photos or videos of the product issue through WhatsApp. Products must be unused, in original condition and with original packaging.
